Converting Units: Animal Weights and Medication Dosages

As a veterinary assistant, you'll regularly calculate medication dosages based on an animal's body weight. Most veterinary drugs are dosed in mg per kg, but scales in many clinics display weight in pounds. Getting this conversion right is critical — an error can harm a patient. In this lesson, you'll practice the exact conversions you'll use on the job every day.
